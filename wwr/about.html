History

For decades, since its early beginnings in the late 1970s, Whitewater Rafting Co. has grown from a small circle of river lovers into a trusted name for adventure seekers who crave a deeper connection with nature. Built on a foundation of reliability, respect for the river, and a passion for creating unforgettable moments, the company has guided countless explorers through roaring rapids and serene stretches alike. Over the years, our commitment has remained the same: to offer experiences that bring people closer to the wild beauty around them while helping them forge new memories that last long after the water settles.

As we look to the future, Whitewater Rafting Co. continues to evolve while staying true to the values that shaped us. Our guides bring not only technical expertise but a genuine love for the outdoors, creating an atmosphere where safety, camaraderie, and adventure come together on every trip.
